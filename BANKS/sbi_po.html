Eligibility Criteria

Nationality

As per the eligibility criteria of SBI PO 2023, only Indians can apply for the SBI PO exam.

Age Limit

As mentioned in the eligibility criteria, the minimum SBI PO age limit is 21 years while the maximum is 30 years. Candidates must have been born not later than April 1, 2001, and not earlier than April 2, 1992. There is upper-age relaxation given to candidates who belong to reserved categories. In the following table, aspirants can check the category-wise upper-age relaxation.

Category Age Relaxation
SC/ST 5 years
SBI PO age limit OBC relaxation (non-creamy layer) 3 years
PWD SC/ST 15 years
PWD OBC 13 years
PWD General 10 years
Ex-Servicemen, Commissioned officers including Emergency Commissioned Officers (ECOs)/Short Service Commissioned Officers (SSCOs) 5 years

Academic Qualification

According to the SBI PO 2023 eligibility criteria, the academic qualification required is graduation in any discipline (BA, B.Com, B.Sc, BBA, BCA, B.Tech, etc.) from a recognised university. Aspirants in the Final Year/Semester of graduation can also apply; however, they will have to produce proof of having passed the graduation examination during the interview round.

Number of Attempts

There is a specific number of attempts that aspirants are entitled to in SBI PO. As per the official SBI PO eligibility criteria 2023, we have shared the category-wise maximum number of attempts.

Category Maximum Number of Attempts
General / EWS 4
General (PwBD) / EWS (PwBD) 7
OBC 7
OBC (PwBD) 7
SC/SC (PwBD)/ST/ST (PwBD) No restriction
