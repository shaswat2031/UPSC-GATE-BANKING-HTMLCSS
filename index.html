GATE

The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is a national-level examination in India jointly conducted by IITs and IISc. It evaluates understanding of engineering and science subjects and is used for postgraduate admissions and job opportunities. GATE assesses knowledge, problem-solving skills, and application of concepts. Scores are valid for two years and are widely recognized for technical expertise.

UPSC

UPSC is a vital civil service exam in India, recruiting for IAS, IPS, and IFS. It includes Prelims, Mains, and an Interview to assess knowledge and decision-making. Successful candidates serve in key government roles.
